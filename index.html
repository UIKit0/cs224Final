Papers:

  • Gooch, B., Sloan, P., & Gooch, A. (1999). Interactive technical illustration. Proceedings of the 1999 Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics, 31–38. doi:10.1145/300523.300526. Retrieved from http://www.ppsloan.org/publications/iti99.pdf

  • McGuire, M., & Fein, A. (2006). Real-time rendering of cartoon smoke and clouds. Proceedings of the 4th International Symposium on Non-Photorealistic Animation and Rendering. Retrieved from http://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?id=1124733

  • Choi, J.-J., & Lee, H.-J. (2006). Rendering stylized highlights using projective textures. The Visual Computer, 22(9-11), 805–813. doi:10.1007/s00371-006-0070-y. Retrieved from http://link.springer.com/article/10.1007%2Fs00371-006-0070-y

  • McGuire, M. (2006). A real-time, controllable simulator for plausible smoke, 1–4. Retrieved from http://graphics.cs.williams.edu/papers/SmokeSimBrown06/smoke-simulation-brown06.pdf

Description:

We plan to implement a non-photorealistic shader based on Gooch et al. [1999] with augmented stylized highlights Choi et. al [2006]. As well, we will implement a toon smoke effect and the smoke’s physical simulation using PhysX (Nvidia’s GPU accelerated physics engine). The paper McGuire et al. [2006] uses ODE, but it shouldn’t be too bad to use PhysX instead. If time allows we would like to improve the toon shaders’ stroke by taking into consideration parts of Goodwin et. al [2007].
